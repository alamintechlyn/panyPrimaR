Passion Award

The Penny Prima Passion Award is awarded to a soloist in the petite age division whose passion for the art of dance is exuded through their performance from the moment they enter the stage to the moment they leave.

Dancers in all competitive levels are eligible and the winner will be awarded a plaque and a special collection of Penny Prima toys and books.
